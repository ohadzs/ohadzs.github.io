Evolution of Ideas in Networking. That standard we talked about - the smart guys how are thinking about it are writing and publishing something called RFC. This document specifics exactly how the protocol should work. Going over RFC 7432 you learn about EVPN - L2VPN solution which is much more simple, easy to understand, much more beautiful then RFC 4761/2 - much older solution for the same problem. the older one took the principles of L2 networks (the 5 I have not explained about earlier) and implemented them on WAN (L3) networks, to spread something over something, you would not understand, sorry. The point is this- without RFC 4761/2, the client and the network engineer would never get to RFC 7432. Ideas develop, grow. At first we think “oh we only need a physical small thingy that will allow us to make a phone calls from remote locations” after some time, experiment, demand (money) and good load of thinking - we got the the latest iPhones and I don’t need to tell you about their capabilities.
